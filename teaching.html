Teaching

I currently teach three courses as an Associate Professor at the Faculty of Electrical Engineering and Information Technologies part of the University of Ss. Cyril and Methodius in Skopje, Macedonia. The lectures (in Macedonian) and the code for all the courses can be found on GitLab, as well as GitHub.


Digital Audio Processing

The course focuses on the analysis, processing, compression and synthesis of digital audio signals and speech.

https://gitlab.com/feeit-freecourseware/digital-audio-processing

Biomedical Electronics

The course focuses on human physiology, electronic measurement devices and algorithms for processing of biomedical signals.

https://gitlab.com/feeit-freecourseware/biomedical-electronics

 Electroacoustics

The course focuses on the physics of sound, electroacoustic analogies, psychoacoustics, room acoustics, microphones, and loudspeakers.

https://gitlab.com/feeit-freecourseware/electroacoustics
