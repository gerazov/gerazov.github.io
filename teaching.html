I currently teach three courses as Assistant Professor at the Faculty of Electrical Engineering and Information Technologies part of the University of Ss. Cyril and Methodius in Skopje, Macedonia. The lectures (in Macedonian) and the code for all the courses can be found on GitHub.

Digital Audio Systems

The course focuses on the analysis, processing, compression and synthesis of digital audio signals.

https://github.com/FEEIT-FreeCourseWare/Digital-Audio-Systems

 Electroacoustics

The course focuses on the physics of sound, electroacoustic analogies, psychoacoustics, room acoustics, microphones, and loudspeakers.

https://github.com/FEEIT-FreeCourseWare/Electroacoustics

Biomedical Electronics

The course focuses on human physiology, electronic measurement devices and algorithms for processing of biomedical signals.

https://github.com/FEEIT-FreeCourseWare/Biomedical_Electronics
