Career Profile

My expertise is machine learning, deep learning, and signal processing. I am enthusiastic about doing research and practical implementation and deployment of advanced machine learning and signal processing pipelines, as well as passing my knowledge to my students. I have extensive experience in speech and audio processing, speech synthesis and recognition, speaker recognition, and biomedical signals and electronics.
